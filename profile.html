Profile

Manual is a design and visual communication studio. Our work strives to uncover the intangible essence of a brand and express it through unique visual solutions. Through our process of conceptual design thinking, we help businesses and organizations articulate their unique offering, giving them more value and distinction.

While we are passionate about design and continually push ourselves and our clients, we like to be collaborative and flexible in our approach. We work with a broad range of clients—from startups to the world’s most revered brands—and maintain a consistently high level of execution and production across print, packaging, and digital media.

Partners

Tom Crabtree

Creative Director / Founder

Born in Yorkshire, England, Tom began his career in London, working for highly acclaimed design studios working in art, architecture, culture, and fashion. In 2006 Tom relocated to California to work for Apple as an Art Director, specializing in packaging design. While at Apple he was creatively involved in packaging, retail, and events for major product launches, most notably the iPhone. In 2009 he founded Manual. In a short time Manual has built an international reputation for producing intelligently crafted design for clients in technology, retail, and culture. Tom's design work and writing has been widely published in design books and magazines worldwide. Linkedin Profile >

Patricia Callaway

Account Director / Partner

Originally from North Carolina, Patricia’s career in marketing and communications spans both sides of the pond. Patricia has enjoyed working with creative people in many industries, but namely publishing, the arts, and technology. Since 2009, she has run the business side of Manual, working directly with clients and collaborators to realize projects. Linkedin Profile
